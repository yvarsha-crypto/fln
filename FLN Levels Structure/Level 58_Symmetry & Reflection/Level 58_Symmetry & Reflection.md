# **Level 58: Symmetry & Reflection**

**Class:** Class 4 | **Age Group:** Age 9-10

## **Objective**

Develop the ability to identify, draw, and complete symmetrical figures while understanding lines of symmetry and reflection through visual representations.

---

## **Description**

Students learn that symmetry occurs when a figure can be divided into two equal mirror-image halves. They identify lines of symmetry, complete symmetrical figures, recognize symmetrical objects, and explore reflections using pictures and geometric shapes.

---

## **Learning Outcomes**

Students can:

* Identify symmetrical and non-symmetrical figures.  
* Draw the line of symmetry.  
* Complete the missing half of a symmetrical figure.  
* Recognize reflection in simple shapes.  
* Identify symmetry in everyday objects.

---

## **Topics Covered**

* Symmetry  
* Line of Symmetry  
* Reflection  
* Mirror Images  
* Symmetrical Figures  
* Non-Symmetrical Figures

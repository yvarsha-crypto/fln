# Level 29: Division — Introduction

## Concept
Division as equal sharing and equal grouping.

## Objective
Understand division as sharing equally and grouping, and connect it to multiplication.

## Description
Students learn division through equal sharing (distribute objects among groups) and equal grouping (how many groups can be made). The connection to multiplication (÷ as reverse of ×) is highlighted. Divide within 20 using tables of 2, 3, 4, 5.

## Learning Outcome
- Share objects equally among groups.
- Identify how many groups can be formed.
- Solve simple division problems using objects and pictures.

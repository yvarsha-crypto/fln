# Level 31: Time

## Concept
Reading time on a clock (hour and half-hour); understanding days of the week and months of the year.

## Objective
Read time to the hour and half-hour; name and order days and months.

## Description
Students learn to read an analogue clock at o'clock and half past. They sequence days of the week and months of the year, and connect time to daily routines.

## Learning Outcome
- Read time to the hour (3 o'clock) and half-hour (half past 3).
- Name and write days of the week in order.
- Name months of the year and identify seasons.

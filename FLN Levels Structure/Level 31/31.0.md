**Main Level: 31.0**

### SECTION A: Read the clock
Clock faces shown at o'clock and half past times. Child writes the time.
Example: [clock showing 3:00] → ___ o'clock

### SECTION B: Draw the hands
Child draws clock hands for given times: 5 o'clock, half past 7.

### SECTION C: Days of the week
Fill in the missing days: Sunday, ___, Tuesday, ___, Thursday, ___, Saturday.

### SECTION D: Months
Write the months of the year. Which is the 6th month? Which comes after August?

# Level 38: Ordering (Ascending & Descending)

**Class:** Class 3 | **Age Group:** Age 8-9

## **Objective**

Develop the ability to arrange three-digit numbers in ascending and descending order using place value understanding.

## **Description**

Students learn to arrange numbers up to 1000 from smallest to largest (ascending order) and largest to smallest (descending order). 

They use place value concepts to determine the correct order of numbers and strengthen their understanding of numerical relationships.

## **Learning Outcome**

Students can:

* Arrange numbers in ascending order.  
* Arrange numbers in descending order.  
* Identify the smallest and largest numbers.  
* Apply place value to order three-digit numbers.  
* Complete ordered number sequences.

## **Topics Covered**

* Ascending Order  
* Descending Order  
* Place Value  
* Smallest Number  
* Largest Number  
* Number Sequencing

## **Example Activities**

* Arrange numbers from smallest to largest.  
* Arrange numbers from largest to smallest.  
* Identify the smallest and largest numbers.  
* Complete ordered sequences

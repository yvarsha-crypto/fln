## **Level 59: Advanced Mastery Assessment**

**Class:** Review | **Age Group:** -

### **Objective**

Assess students' understanding and application of advanced mathematical concepts covered in Levels 49–58 through a comprehensive, integrated assessment.

---

### **Description**

This assessment evaluates students' mastery of advanced mathematical concepts, including four-digit numbers, multiplication, division, factors and multiples, fraction operations, decimals, area and perimeter, angles, maps and directions, and symmetry. Students demonstrate conceptual understanding, computational fluency, spatial reasoning, and problem-solving skills through a variety of visual and numerical activities.

---

### **Learning Outcomes**

Students can:

* Apply place value concepts up to 10,000.  
* Solve advanced multiplication and division problems.  
* Identify factors and multiples.  
* Perform operations with fractions.  
* Interpret and compare decimals.  
* Calculate area and perimeter.  
* Identify and compare angles.  
* Interpret maps and directions.  
* Recognize and apply symmetry.  
* Integrate multiple mathematical concepts to solve problems.

---

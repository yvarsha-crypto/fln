# Level 35: Review Assessment

**Class:** Review | **Age Group:** -

## **Objective**

Assess students' mastery of all concepts learned from Levels 24–34 through a comprehensive assessment covering place value, number operations, measurement, and real-life mathematical concepts.

## **Description**

This level serves as a comprehensive review assessment of all concepts introduced from **Levels 24–34**. 

Students solve a variety of visual and numerical problems that evaluate their understanding of place value, numbers, operations, measurement, time, data handling, and multiplication. 

The assessment focuses on concept mastery and helps identify areas where additional support may be required.

## **Learning Outcome**

Students demonstrate competency in:

* Place Value (Tens & Ones)  
* Numbers 51–100  
* Carry Addition  
* Borrow Subtraction  
* Comparison  
* Ordering  
* Data Handling using Tally Marks  
* Reading Time  
* Ordinal Positions  
* Multiplication through Repeated Addition  
* Measurement (Length, Weight & Capacity)

## **Topics Covered**

* Place Value (Tens & Ones)  
* Numbers 51–100  
* Carry Addition  
* Borrow Subtraction  
* Comparison (\>, \<, \=)  
* Ascending & Descending Order  
* Tally Marks  
* Reading Analog Clocks  
* Ordinal Positions (1st–10th)  
* Multiplication using Repeated Addition (Tables 2, 3 & 4\)  
* Length  
* Weight  
* Capacity

# Level 36: Division — Facts and Word Problems

## Concept
Division facts connected to multiplication; division of 2-digit numbers by 1-digit; word problems.

## Objective
Use multiplication facts to divide; divide 2-digit numbers by 1-digit; solve word problems.

## Description
Building on Level 29 (intro division), students now use multiplication tables to recall division facts, divide two-digit numbers, and solve word problems. The relationship ÷ × are reinforced.

## Learning Outcome
- Recall division facts using multiplication tables.
- Divide 2-digit numbers by 1-digit (with and without remainders).
- Solve division word problems.

# **Level 49: Numbers up to 10,000**

**Class:** Class 4 | **Age Group:** Age 9-10

## **Objective**

Develop an understanding of four-digit numbers by identifying thousands, hundreds, tens, and ones, representing numbers in standard and expanded forms, and applying place value concepts to numbers up to 10,000.

## **Description**

Students learn to recognize, read, write, and represent numbers from **1,001 to 10,000**. 

They identify place values up to the thousands place, build numbers using place value, write numbers in expanded form, complete number sequences, and identify missing numbers. 

Activities strengthen number sense and prepare students for advanced arithmetic with larger numbers.

## **Learning Outcomes**

Students can:

* Recognize and read numbers up to 10,000.  
* Identify thousands, hundreds, tens, and ones.  
* Write numbers in standard and expanded forms.  
* Build numbers using place values.  
* Complete number sequences.  
* Identify missing numbers.  
* Apply place value concepts to four-digit numbers.

## **Topics Covered**

* Numbers 1,001–10,000  
* Thousands, Hundreds, Tens & Ones  
* Place Value  
* Standard Form  
* Expanded Form  
* Number Formation  
* Missing Numbers  
* Number Sequences

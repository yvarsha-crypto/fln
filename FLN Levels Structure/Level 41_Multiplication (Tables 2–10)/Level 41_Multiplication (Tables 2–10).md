# Level 41: Multiplication (Tables 2–10)

**Class:** Class 3 | **Age Group:** Age 8-9

## **Objective**

Develop fluency in multiplication using tables from 2 to 10 and apply multiplication strategies to solve multiplication problems involving one-digit and two-digit numbers.

## **Description**

Students strengthen multiplication skills by learning multiplication tables from 2 to 10 and progressively applying them to multiplication problems. 

They practice multiplication facts, complete multiplication patterns, and perform vertical multiplication with increasing confidence.

## **Learning Outcome**

Students can:

* Recall multiplication tables from 2–10.  
* Apply multiplication facts accurately.  
* Multiply one-digit numbers.  
* Multiply two-digit numbers by one-digit numbers.  
* Apply place value while multiplying.

## **Topics Covered**

* Multiplication Tables (2–10)  
* Vertical Multiplication  
* Two-Digit × One-Digit Multiplication

# **Level 51: Advanced Division**

**Class:** Class 4 | **Age Group:** Age 9-10

## **Objective**

Develop proficiency in dividing larger numbers by applying place value concepts and the standard long division algorithm to solve division problems involving two-digit and three-digit numbers.

## **Description**

Students extend their understanding of division by solving two-digit and three-digit division problems using the standard long division method. 

They divide numbers with and without remainders and apply division to solve practical situations.

## **Learning Outcomes**

Students can:

* Divide two-digit numbers by one-digit numbers.  
* Divide three-digit numbers by one-digit numbers.  
* Solve division problems with and without remainders.  
* Apply place value during long division.  
* Solve real-life division problems.

## **Topics Covered**

* Long Division  
* Two-Digit ÷ One-Digit  
* Three-Digit ÷ One-Digit  
* Division with Remainders  
* Division Word Problems


# Level 14: Counting and Tracing

**Class:** Class 1 | **Age Group:** Age 6-7

### SECTION A: Matching (Object ↔ Number)

## Objective

Match quantities with numerals.

## Description

In this level, children match quantities of objects with their corresponding numerals and engage in creative tracing activities to develop fine motor skills.

## Learning Outcome

Students connect objects with numbers.

## Topics Covered

* Matching Quantities to Numerals
* Counting
* Tracing
* Fine Motor Skills

## Skills Developed

Counting

Matching

### SECTION B: Fun – Draw by Tracing

## Objective

Learning through creative activities.

Activities

Trace animals

Trace shapes

Trace objects

Trace letters

Trace paths

## Skills Developed

Creativity

Fine motor control

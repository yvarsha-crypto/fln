# Level 34: Three-Digit Addition and Subtraction

## Concept
Addition and subtraction of three-digit numbers with carrying/borrowing; word problems.

## Objective
Add and subtract three-digit numbers accurately; solve one- and two-step word problems.

## Description
Building on Level 27, students now operate with hundreds. Column addition with carrying across tens and hundreds, and subtraction with borrowing across columns. Word problems set in real-life contexts are central.

## Learning Outcome
- Add three-digit numbers with carrying.
- Subtract three-digit numbers with borrowing.
- Solve word problems involving 3-digit addition and subtraction.

# Level 1: Quantity  Comparison

**Class:** Preschool 1 | **Age Group:** Age 3-4

## Objective

Comparing Quantities (Equal, More, Less)

## Description

In this level child observes two group of objects and determine whether they contain the same quantity, greater or less.

No counting is required; only visual observations

## Learning Outcome

By the end of this level, the child will be able to:

Recognize equal quantities

Identify groups with more or less objects

Compare quantities visually.

Develop observation and comparison skills.

Build a foundation for future mathematical operations.

## Topics Covered

* Comparing Quantities
* Equal, More and Less
* Visual Comparison

## Skills Developed

### Cognitive Skills

Visual discrimination

Quantity recognition

Logical thinking

Comparison skills

### Mathematical Skills

Understanding equal, more and less

Quantity awareness

### Foundational Skills

Attention to detail

Decision making

Pattern observation

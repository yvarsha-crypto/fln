**Main Level: 24.0**
Every student must clear this before moving to Level 25.

### SECTION A: Shape Identification
Child is shown pictures of real-world objects (ball, book, window, pizza slice) and must circle/match the correct shape name: Circle / Square / Rectangle / Triangle.

### SECTION B: Spatial Position
Pictures show objects in different positions. Child must circle the correct word:
- The cat is **inside / outside** the box.
- The bird is **above / below** the tree.
- The ball is **near / far** from the bat.

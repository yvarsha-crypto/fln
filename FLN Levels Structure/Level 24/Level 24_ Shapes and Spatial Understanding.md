# Level 24: Shapes and Spatial Understanding

## Concept
Identifying and naming basic 2D shapes; understanding spatial relationships (inside/outside, above/below, near/far).

## Objective
Recognise, name, and describe basic shapes and understand position in space.

## Description
Students observe real-world objects and match them to 2D shapes (circle, square, rectangle, triangle). They also learn spatial vocabulary — inside/outside, above/below, left/right, near/far — through picture-based activities.

## Learning Outcome
- Identify and name circle, square, rectangle, and triangle.
- Sort objects by shape.
- Use spatial words to describe position of objects.

## Skills Developed
### Cognitive
Visual discrimination, spatial reasoning, classification.
### Mathematical
Shape recognition, geometry foundation.

# Level 44: Time & Calendar

**Class:** Class 3 | **Age Group:** Age 8-9

## **Objective**

Develop the ability to read clocks and calendars, identify dates, days, months, and apply time concepts in everyday situations.

## **Description**

Students strengthen their understanding of time by reading analog clocks, interpreting calendars, identifying days and months, and solving simple time-based activities. The level emphasizes visual recognition and practical application through clocks and calendars.

## **Learning Outcome**

Students can:

* Read analog clocks.  
* Identify hours, half-hours, quarter past, and quarter to.  
* Read and interpret a calendar.  
* Identify days of the week and months of the year.  
* Determine dates, weekdays, and simple time sequences.

## **Topics Covered**

* Analog Clock  
* Hours & Minutes  
* Quarter Past  
* Quarter To  
* Calendar  
* Days of the Week  
* Months of the Year  
* Dates

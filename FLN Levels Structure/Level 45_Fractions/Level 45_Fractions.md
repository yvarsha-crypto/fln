# Level 45: Fractions

**Class:** Class 3 | **Age Group:** Age 8-9

## **Objective**

Develop an understanding of fractions as equal parts of a whole through visual representations and simple fraction concepts.

## **Description**

Students learn that a whole can be divided into equal parts and that each part represents a fraction. 

Using pictures of everyday objects and shapes, students identify, compare, and represent simple fractions such as one-half, one-third, one-fourth, two-thirds, and three-fourths.

## **Learning Outcome**

Students can:

* Recognize fractions as equal parts of a whole.  
* Identify simple fractions from pictures.  
* Represent fractions using visual models.  
* Compare simple fractions.  
* Match fractions with corresponding visual representations.

## **Topics Covered**

* Whole  
* Equal Parts  
* Half (½)  
* One-Third (⅓)  
* One-Fourth (¼)  
* Two-Thirds (⅔)  
* Three-Fourths (¾)  
* Fraction Representation  
* Fraction Comparison

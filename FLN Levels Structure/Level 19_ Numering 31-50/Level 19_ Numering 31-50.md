# Level 19: Numbering 31-50

**Class:** Class 1 | **Age Group:** Age 6-7

## Objective

Recognize, write, and sequence numbers from 31 to 50 and match them with their number names.

## SECTION A: NUMBERS
Objective
Writing, and sequencing of numbers from 31 to 50.

## SECTION B: NUMBER NAMES
In this section the child needs to match or select the correct number name for each number. 

## Description
In this level, there will be 2 sections, in SECTION A students are introduced to numbers from 31 to 50. 
Children practice identifying, reading, writing, and sequencing these numbers through engaging activities such as tracing, number writing, missing number exercises, and number arrangement tasks. In SECTION B, the child will recognize number names.

## Learning Outcome
Students can identify, read, write, and arrange numbers from 31 to 50 correctly.

## Topics Covered

* Number Recognition (31-50)
* Number Writing
* Number Sequencing
* Number Names

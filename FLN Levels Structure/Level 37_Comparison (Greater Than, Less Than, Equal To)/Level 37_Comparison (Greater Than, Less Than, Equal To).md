# Level 37: Comparison (Greater Than, Less Than, Equal To)

**Class:** Class 3 | **Age Group:** Age 8-9

## **Objective**

Develop the ability to compare numbers up to 1000 using place value and the symbols greater than (\>), less than (\<), and equal to (=).

## **Description**

Students compare three-digit numbers by examining their hundreds, tens, and ones. 

They identify the greater, smaller, or equal number and apply place value understanding to compare numbers in different situations.

## **Learning Outcome**

Students can:

* Compare numbers up to 1000\.  
* Use \>, \< and \= correctly.  
* Identify the greater and smaller number.  
* Compare numbers using hundreds, tens, and ones.  
* Apply comparison skills to number sequences.

## **Topics Covered**

* Greater Than (\>)  
* Less Than (\<)  
* Equal To (=)  
* Comparing Three-Digit Numbers  
* Place Value Comparison

## **Example Activities**

* Insert the correct comparison symbol.  
* Circle the greater number.  
* Circle the smaller number.  
* Compare numbers using place value.

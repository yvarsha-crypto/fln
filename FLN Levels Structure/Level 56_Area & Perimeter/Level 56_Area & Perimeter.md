# **Level 56: Area & Perimeter**

**Class:** Class 4 | **Age Group:** Age 9-10

## **Objective**

Develop an understanding of area and perimeter by measuring, calculating, and comparing the space inside shapes and the distance around shapes using standard units.

---

## **Description**

Students learn to distinguish between area and perimeter through visual models and practical activities. They calculate the perimeter of simple shapes by adding side lengths and determine the area by counting square units and applying basic formulas for rectangles and squares.

---

## **Learning Outcomes**

Students can:

* Differentiate between area and perimeter.  
* Calculate the perimeter of squares and rectangles.  
* Calculate the area of rectangles and squares.  
* Count square units to determine area.  
* Compare the areas and perimeters of different shapes.  
* Apply area and perimeter to real-life situations.

---

## **Topics Covered**

* Area  
* Perimeter  
* Square Units  
* Rectangle  
* Square  
* Measuring Length  
* Real-Life Applications

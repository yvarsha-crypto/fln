# Level 43: Standard Measurement & Simple Conversions

**Class:** Class 3 | **Age Group:** Age 8-9

## **Objective**

Develop an understanding of standard units of measurement and apply simple measurement conversions in everyday situations.

## **Description**

Students learn to measure and compare objects using standard units of length, weight, and capacity. 

They recognize measuring tools, read simple measurements, and perform basic conversions between commonly used units through visual and practical activities.

## **Learning Outcome**

Students can:

* Identify standard units of measurement.  
* Select the appropriate unit for an object.  
* Read simple rulers, weighing scales, and measuring jars.  
* Perform basic unit conversions.  
* Compare measurements using standard units.

## **Topics Covered**

* Length (cm, m)  
* Weight (g, kg)  
* Capacity (mL, L)  
* Measuring Tools  
* Standard Units  
* Simple Unit Conversions

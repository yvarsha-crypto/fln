# Level 27: Addition and Subtraction up to 100

## Concept
Two-digit addition (with carrying) and subtraction (with borrowing), including simple word problems.

## Objective
Solve two-digit addition and subtraction problems up to 100, with and without carrying/borrowing.

## Description
Students extend their arithmetic skills from Level 16/17 (up to 30, no carry/borrow) to full two-digit operations up to 100. Both column-form and horizontal problems are used. Simple word problems in context are introduced.

## Learning Outcome
- Add two-digit numbers with carrying.
- Subtract two-digit numbers with borrowing.
- Solve one-step word problems involving addition/subtraction.

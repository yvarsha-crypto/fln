# Level 40: Subtraction (Up to 1000)

**Class:** Class 3 | **Age Group:** Age 8-9

## **Objective**

Develop fluency in subtracting numbers up to 1000 using place value, with and without regrouping (borrowing).

## **Description**

Students learn to subtract one-digit, two-digit, and three-digit numbers using vertical subtraction. 

They apply place value concepts to solve subtraction problems with and without regrouping and strengthen their understanding of subtraction through progressively simpler activities.

## **Learning Outcome**

Students can:

* Subtract numbers up to 1000\.  
* Solve subtraction problems with and without regrouping.  
* Apply place value while subtracting.  
* Subtract numbers with different digit lengths.  
* Verify subtraction answers.

## **Topics Covered**

* Subtraction without Regrouping  
* Subtraction with Regrouping  
* Vertical Subtraction  
* Place Value  
* Mixed-Digit Subtraction

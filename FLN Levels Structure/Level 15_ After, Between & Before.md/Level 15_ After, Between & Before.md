# Level 15: After, Between & Before

**Class:** Class 1 | **Age Group:** Age 6-7

## Objective
Check the concept of after numbers, before numbers, and between numbers.

## Description

In this level, children practice identifying numbers that come before, after, and between given numbers in a sequence, reinforcing their understanding of number order.

## Learning Outcome
Students can identify numbers that come before, after, and between given numbers.

## Topics Covered

* Before Numbers
* After Numbers
* Between Numbers
* Number Sequencing

## Skills Developed
Number sequencing
Logical reasoning
Number recognition

# Level 27: Borrow Subtraction

**Class:** Class 2 | **Age Group:** Age 7-8

### **Objective**

Develop fluency in subtraction involving regrouping (borrowing) within 100 and apply subtraction strategies to solve numerical and real-life problems.

### **Description**

Students learn to subtract numbers that require borrowing from the tens place. 

They develop a strong understanding of place value while practicing subtraction through structured and progressively simpler activities.

### **Learning Outcome**

Students can:

* Subtract numbers requiring borrowing.  
* Understand regrouping of tens into ones.  
* Solve subtraction problems accurately within 100\.  
* Apply borrowing strategies in real-life situations.

**Topics Covered**

* Place Value  
* Borrow Subtraction  
* Regrouping Tens and Ones

### **Example Activities**

* Solve vertical subtraction sums requiring borrowing.

**Main Level: 25.0**

### SECTION A: Length Comparison
Child looks at pairs of pictures and circles the longer/taller object.
Examples: pencil vs crayon, tree vs flower, building vs hut.

### SECTION B: Weight Comparison
Child looks at a balance-scale picture and identifies which object is heavier.

### SECTION C: Capacity Comparison
Child looks at two containers (big jar vs small cup) and identifies which holds more.

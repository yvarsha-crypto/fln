# Level 25: Measurement — Introduction

## Concept
Comparing lengths, weights, and volumes using non-standard units and direct comparison.

## Objective
Compare and order objects by length, weight, and capacity without using standard units.

## Description
Students compare objects directly (longer/shorter, heavier/lighter, more/less) through picture-based and hands-on activities. Non-standard units (hand spans, cubes, cups) are introduced.

## Learning Outcome
- Compare two objects as longer/shorter, taller/shorter.
- Compare two objects as heavier/lighter.
- Compare two containers as holding more/less.

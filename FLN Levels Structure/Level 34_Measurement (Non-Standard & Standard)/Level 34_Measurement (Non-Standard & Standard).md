# Level 34: Measurement (Non-Standard & Standard)

**Class:** Class 2 | **Age Group:** Age 7-8

## **Objective**

Develop an understanding of measuring and comparing the length, weight, and capacity of objects using both non-standard and standard measurement methods.

## **Description**

Students learn to compare and measure objects based on length, weight, and capacity. 

They first use familiar non-standard units such as blocks, cubes, paper clips, and containers, and then recognize standard measuring tools such as rulers, weighing scales, and measuring jars through visual activities.

## **Learning Outcome**

Students can:

* Compare objects based on length, weight, and capacity.  
* Measure objects using non-standard units.  
* Interpret simple measurements using standard measuring tools.  
* Relate measuring tools to everyday objects.  
* Apply measurement concepts in visual situations.

  ## **Topics Covered**

* Length  
* Weight  
* Capacity  
* Non-Standard Measurement  
* Standard Measurement  
* Comparing Measurements

  ## **Example Activities**

* Measure an object using blocks or paper clips.  
* Compare the lengths of two objects.  
* Identify the heavier object using a balance.  
* Compare containers based on the amount they can hold.  
* Read simple measurements from a ruler or scale.

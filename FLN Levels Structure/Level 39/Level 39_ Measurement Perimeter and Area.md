# Level 39: Measurement — Perimeter and Area

## Concept
Understanding and calculating perimeter (sum of sides) and area (counting unit squares) of simple shapes.

## Objective
Find the perimeter of simple shapes by adding side lengths; find area by counting unit squares.

## Description
Students learn perimeter as the total boundary length of a shape (measured by adding all sides). Area is introduced as the number of unit squares that cover a shape. Both are applied to squares, rectangles, and irregular shapes on grid paper.

## Learning Outcome
- Calculate the perimeter of a shape by adding its sides.
- Find area by counting unit squares on a grid.
- Distinguish between perimeter and area.

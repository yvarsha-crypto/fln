# Level 22: Ordering

**Class:** Class 1 | **Age Group:** Age 6-7

## Objective

Arrange numbers in ascending and descending order.

## Description

In this level, students learn to arrange numbers in the correct order by identifying the smallest and largest numbers within a group.

Children practice organizing numbers in ascending order (smallest to largest) and descending order (largest to smallest) through a variety of sorting and sequencing activities.

This level strengthens number sense, comparison skills, and understanding of numerical relationships.

## Learning Outcome

Students can organize numbers from smallest to largest and largest to smallest.

## Topics Covered

* Ascending Order
* Descending Order
* Smallest and Largest Numbers
* Number Sequencing (1-50)

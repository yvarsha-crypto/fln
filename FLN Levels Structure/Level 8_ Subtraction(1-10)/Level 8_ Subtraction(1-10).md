# Level 8: Subtraction(1-10)

**Class:** Preschool 3 | **Age Group:** Age 5-6

# Level 8: Subtraction Through Objects & Numbers

## Objective

Understand subtraction as taking away objects from a group and finding the remaining quantity.

## Description

In this level, students learn subtraction through visual and object-based activities. Students count the objects, identify the quantity being removed, and determine how many objects remain. Activities gradually connect visual representations to numerical subtraction. All subtraction problems are within 10 and do not require borrowing.

Example:

🍎🍎🍎🍎🍎 − 🍎🍎 = ?

___ − ___ = ___

## Learning Outcome

Students can determine the remaining quantity after taking away objects and solve subtraction problems within 10 without borrowing.

## Topics Covered

* Taking Away Objects
* Object-Based Subtraction
* Numerical Subtraction
* Subtraction within 10

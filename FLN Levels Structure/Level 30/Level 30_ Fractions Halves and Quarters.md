# Level 30: Fractions — Halves and Quarters

## Concept
Understanding one-half (½) and one-quarter (¼) through visual and real-life representations.

## Objective
Identify, represent, and compare halves and quarters of shapes and groups of objects.

## Description
Students learn that equal parts of a whole make fractions. They identify halved and quartered shapes, shade given fractions, and apply halves/quarters to groups of objects. Real-life contexts (sharing a pizza, folding paper) are used.

## Learning Outcome
- Identify ½ and ¼ of a shape.
- Shade ½ and ¼ of given figures.
- Find ½ and ¼ of small collections (e.g. ½ of 8 = 4).

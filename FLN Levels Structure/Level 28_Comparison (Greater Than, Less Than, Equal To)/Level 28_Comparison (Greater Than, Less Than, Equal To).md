# Level 28: Comparison (Greater Than, Less Than, Equal To)

**Class:** Class 2 | **Age Group:** Age 7-8

### **Objective**

Develop the ability to compare numbers using mathematical symbols (\> , \< , \=) and understand numerical relationships within 100\.

### **Description**

Students learn to compare numbers and determine whether a number is greater than, less than, or equal to another number. 

They apply place value understanding and number sense to make accurate comparisons in different contexts.

### **Learning Outcome**

Students can:

* Compare numbers using \>, \<, and \= symbols.  
* Identify the greater and smaller number.  
* Determine if two numbers are equal.  
* Use place value to compare two-digit numbers.  
* Apply comparison skills in real-life situations.

### **Topics Covered**

* Greater Than (\>)  
* Less Than (\<)  
* Equal To (=)  
* Comparing Two-Digit Numbers  
* Place Value Comparison  
* Real-Life Comparison Problems

### **Example Activities**

* Insert the correct comparison symbol.  
* Circle the greater number.  
* Circle the smaller number.  
* Compare quantities shown in pictures.

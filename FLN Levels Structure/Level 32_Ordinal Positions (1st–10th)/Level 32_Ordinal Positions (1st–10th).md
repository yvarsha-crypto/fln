# Level 32: Ordinal Positions (1st–10th)

**Class:** Class 2 | **Age Group:** Age 7-8

### **Objective**

Develop the ability to identify and use ordinal positions from 1st to 10th.

### **Description**

Students learn to recognize the position of objects in a sequence using ordinal numbers. 

Activities focus on identifying, writing, and locating positions visually rather than relying on reading text.

### **Learning Outcome**

Students can:

* Identify positions from 1st to 10th.  
* Write ordinal positions.  
* Locate objects based on their position.  
* Understand order in a sequence.  
* Apply positional concepts in visual situations.

### **Topics Covered**

* 1st–10th Positions  
* Identifying Position  
* Writing Position  
* Locating Objects  
* Ordering Objects

# Level 35: Multiplication — Tables and Word Problems

## Concept
Multiplication tables up to 10; multiplication of 2-digit by 1-digit numbers; word problems.

## Objective
Recall multiplication facts (tables 2–10); multiply 2-digit by 1-digit; solve word problems.

## Description
Building on Level 28 (intro to multiplication), students now memorise and apply tables 2–10, multiply 2-digit numbers by a single digit, and solve real-world word problems involving multiplication.

## Learning Outcome
- Recall multiplication tables 2–10.
- Multiply a 2-digit number by a 1-digit number.
- Solve multiplication word problems.

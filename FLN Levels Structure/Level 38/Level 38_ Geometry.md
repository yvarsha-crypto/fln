# Level 38: Geometry — Lines, Angles, and Shapes

## Concept
Straight/curved lines, right angles, and properties of basic 2D shapes (sides, corners); introduction to 3D shapes.

## Objective
Identify straight and curved lines, right angles, and describe properties of 2D and 3D shapes.

## Description
Students revisit 2D shapes (from Level 24) with more focus on properties: number of sides, corners, and whether angles are right angles. They are introduced to 3D shapes (cube, cuboid, cylinder, cone, sphere) and describe their faces, edges, and vertices.

## Learning Outcome
- Identify and draw straight and curved lines.
- Recognise right angles in shapes and surroundings.
- Describe 2D shapes by sides and corners; 3D shapes by faces, edges, vertices.

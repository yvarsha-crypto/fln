# Level 31: Time

**Class:** Class 2 | **Age Group:** Age 7-8

### **Objective**

Develop the ability to read and interpret time using an analog clock.

### **Description**

Students learn to identify and read time shown on an analog clock. They observe the positions of the hour and minute hands and write the correct time. 

Activities progress from reading full hours to half-hours and quarter-hours.

### **Learning Outcome**

Students can:

* Identify the hour and minute hands.  
* Read time shown on an analog clock.  
* Write time in hours and minutes.  
* Differentiate between full hours, half-hours, and quarter-hours.  
* Relate clock readings to everyday situations.

### **Topics Covered**

* Full Hours  
* Half Hours  
* Quarter Hours  
* Reading Analog Clocks  
* Hour Hand and Minute Hand  
* Writing Time

### **Example Activities**

* Observe a clock and write the time.  
* Match clocks with the correct time.  
* Identify clocks showing the same time.  
* Read and write time from analog clocks.

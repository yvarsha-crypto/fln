# Level 26: Numbers 51–100

## Concept
Extending number recognition, reading, writing, and place value up to 100.

## Objective
Recognise, read, write, and order numbers from 51 to 100 and understand their place value.

## Description
Students extend the number line from 50 to 100. Activities include identifying tens and ones in numbers 51–100, writing missing numbers, reading number names, and arranging numbers in order. This completes the Class 2 NCERT/CBSE requirement of numbers up to 100.

## Learning Outcome
- Read and write numbers 51–100.
- Identify tens and ones in two-digit numbers up to 100.
- Arrange numbers 51–100 in ascending and descending order.

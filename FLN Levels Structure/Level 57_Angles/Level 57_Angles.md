# **Level 57: Angles**

**Class:** Class 4 | **Age Group:** Age 9-10

## **Objective**

Develop an understanding of angles by identifying, measuring, comparing, and classifying different types of angles through visual representations and real-life objects.

---

## **Description**

Students learn that an angle is formed when two lines or rays meet at a common point. They identify and classify right, acute, obtuse, and straight angles using pictures, compare angles based on their size, and recognize angles in everyday objects.

---

## **Learning Outcomes**

Students can:

* Recognize different types of angles.  
* Identify acute, right, obtuse, and straight angles.  
* Compare angles based on their size.  
* Identify angles in real-life objects.  
* Measure simple angles using a protractor (introductory level).

---

## **Topics Covered**

* Angle  
* Acute Angle  
* Right Angle  
* Obtuse Angle  
* Straight Angle  
* Comparing Angles  
* Angles in Everyday Life  
* Introduction to Measuring Angles

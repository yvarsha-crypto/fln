# Level 42: Division (Introduction)

**Class:** Class 3 | **Age Group:** Age 8-9

## **Objective**

Develop an understanding of division through equal sharing, equal grouping, and simple division facts using visual representations.

## **Description**

Students are introduced to division as the process of sharing or grouping objects equally. Through pictures, equal groups, and simple numerical expressions, they learn to divide quantities fairly and understand the relationship between multiplication and division.

## **Learning Outcome**

Students can:

* Divide objects into equal groups.  
* Share objects equally among groups.  
* Identify the number of objects in each group.  
* Solve simple division facts.  
* Relate multiplication and division.


## **Topics Covered**

* Equal Sharing  
* Equal Grouping  
* Division Facts  
* Division using Pictures  
* Relationship between Multiplication and Division


## **Example Activities**

* Share objects equally.  
* Complete equal groups.  
* Write the number in each group.  
* Solve simple division facts.  
* Match multiplication and division.

# Level 24: Numbers 51–100

**Class:** Class 2 | **Age Group:** Age 7-8

### **Objective**

Develop number sense and fluency with numbers from 51 to 100 through recognition, sequencing, comparison, and pattern-based activities.

### **Description**

Students explore numbers from 51 to 100 and strengthen their understanding of place value, number order, and number relationships. 

### **Learning Outcome**

Students can:

* Read and write numbers from 51–100.  
* Identify missing numbers in a sequence.

### **Topics Covered**

* Number Recognition (51–100)  
* Missing Numbers

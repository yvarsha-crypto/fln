# **Level 53: Factors & Multiples**

**Class:** Class 4 | **Age Group:** Age 9-10

## **Objective**

Develop an understanding of factors and multiples by identifying, listing, and applying them to numbers through multiplication and division relationships.

## **Description**

Students learn to identify factors and multiples of whole numbers using multiplication facts and division concepts. 

They recognize common factors, distinguish between factors and multiples, and apply these concepts through number patterns and reasoning activities.

## **Learning Outcomes**

Students can:

* Identify factors of a number.  
* Identify multiples of a number.  
* Distinguish between factors and multiples.  
* Apply multiplication and division to determine factors and multiples.  
* Recognize common factors and common multiples.

## **Topics Covered**

* Factors  
* Multiples  
* Factor Pairs  
* Common Factors  
* Common Multiples  
* Multiplication Relationships

# Level 5: Finger Gesture Counting

**Class:** Preschool 2 | **Age Group:** Age 4-5

## Objective
Connect finger representations with numerals.

## Description

In this level, children learn to associate finger gestures with the corresponding numerals, reinforcing one-to-one correspondence and number recognition through a familiar physical representation.

## Learning Outcome

Students can connect finger gestures to numbers and recognize quantities through finger counting.

## Topics Covered

* Finger Counting
* One-to-One Correspondence
* Number Recognition
* Visual-Quantity Association

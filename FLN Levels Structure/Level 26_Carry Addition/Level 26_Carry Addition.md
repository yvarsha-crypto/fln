# Level 26: Carry Addition

**Class:** Class 2 | **Age Group:** Age 7-8

### **Objective**

Develop fluency in addition involving regrouping (carry) within 100 and apply addition strategies to solve numerical and real-life problems.

### **Description**

Students learn to add numbers that require carrying from the ones place to the tens place. They strengthen place value understanding while practicing addition through structured and progressively simpler exercises.

### **Learning Outcome**

Students can:

* Add numbers requiring carrying.  
* Understand regrouping of ones into tens.  
* Solve addition problems accurately within 100\.  
* Apply carry addition to everyday situations.  
* Check the reasonableness of their answers.

### **Topics Covered**

* Place Value  
* Carry Addition  
* Regrouping Tens and Ones

### **Example Activities**

* Solve vertical carry addition sums.  
* Complete missing numbers in addition equations.  
* Match addition expressions with answers.  
* Solve simple story-based addition problems.

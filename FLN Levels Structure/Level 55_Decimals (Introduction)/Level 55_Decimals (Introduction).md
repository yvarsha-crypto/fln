# **Level 55: Decimals (Introduction)**

**Class:** Class 4 | **Age Group:** Age 9-10

## **Objective**

Develop an understanding of decimals by representing tenths and hundredths using visual models, place value, and simple real-life situations.

---

## **Description**

Students are introduced to decimal numbers as another way of representing parts of a whole. They learn to identify tenths and hundredths, read and write decimals, represent decimals using place value, and relate decimals to money and visual models.

---

## **Learning Outcomes**

Students can:

* Recognize decimal numbers.  
* Identify tenths and hundredths.  
* Read and write decimals.  
* Represent decimals using place value.  
* Compare simple decimal numbers.  
* Relate decimals to money and everyday situations.

---

## **Topics Covered**

* Decimal Numbers  
* Tenths  
* Hundredths  
* Decimal Place Value  
* Reading & Writing Decimals  
* Comparing Decimals  
* Decimals in Money

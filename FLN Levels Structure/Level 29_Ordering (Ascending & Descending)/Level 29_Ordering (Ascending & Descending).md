# Level 29: Ordering (Ascending & Descending)

**Class:** Class 2 | **Age Group:** Age 7-8

### **Objective**

Develop the ability to arrange numbers in ascending and descending order using number sense and place value understanding within 100.

### **Description**

Students learn to organize numbers from smallest to largest (ascending order) and largest to smallest (descending order). 

They strengthen sequencing skills and develop an understanding of numerical relationships through ordering activities.

### **Learning Outcome**

Students can:

* Arrange numbers in ascending order.  
* Arrange numbers in descending order.  
* Identify the smallest and largest numbers in a set.  
* Use place value to determine correct order.  
* Apply ordering skills to number patterns and real-life situations.

### **Topics Covered**

* Ascending Order  
* Descending Order  
* Smallest Number  
* Largest Number  
* Number Sequencing  
* Place Value in Ordering

### **Example Activities**

* Arrange numbers from smallest to largest.  
* Arrange numbers from largest to smallest.  
* Identify the first and last number in a sequence.  
* Complete an ordered number pattern.  
* Solve ordering-based word problems.

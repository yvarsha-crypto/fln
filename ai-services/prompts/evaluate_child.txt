EVALUATION LOGIC:

Step 1: ROOT CAUSE CLASSIFICATION
For each wrong answer, identify:
- Conceptual Gap: Child doesn't understand the topic (needs remediation)
- Careless Mistake: Child knows it but made arithmetic error (needs practice)
- Prerequisite Missing: Child failed because foundational skill is absent (needs review of earlier class)

Step 2: MAP WRONG ANSWERS TO FLN LEVELS
For each wrong answer with a conceptual gap or missing prerequisite, map the topic to its FLN level.

Step 3: APPLY MINIMUM FAILURE LEVEL RULE
Collect all FLN levels where child has conceptual gaps or missing prerequisites. Pick the LOWEST level. That is the assigned remediation level.

Example: Fails at Level 3 (Matching) and Level 5 (Finger Counting) → assigned_level = 3.

Step 4: PERFORMANCE BY DIFFICULTY
Analyze how child performed on:
- Easy questions (should score >90%)
- Medium questions (should score >70%)
- Hard questions (should score >50%)
